BEHIND RAOUTE

Discover the vision behind Raoute—an AI-powered travel platform designed to transform the way people explore California. Built with intelligent recommendations, interactive maps, and personalized itineraries, Raoute combines technology and travel into one seamless experience.

Innovation & Vision

DEVELOPER

Raoute is independently designed and developed by a high school student passionate about Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science. The platform showcases skills in UI design, system architecture, AI integration, and travel recommendation, demonstrating how technology can solve real-world problems.

STORY

Planning a trip often means searching across countless websites, maps, and travel guides. Raoute simplifies that process by combining AI-powered recommendations, interactive maps, and intelligent route planning into one seamless travel experience.

PURPOSE

Raoute makes travel planning smarter and more personalized. By understanding each traveler's interests, budget, and schedule, it generates tailored recommendations and optimized itineraries, demonstrating the practical impact of AI on everyday travel.

DEEP-DISH PIZZA 🍕

Deep-dish Pizza is a famous specialty from Chicago. Unlike regular pizza, it has a thick crust and is filled with generous layers of cheese, meat, vegetables, and tomato sauce.

TEXAS BARBECUE 🥩

Beef brisket or ribs are slowly smoked for many hours, giving the meat a rich, smoky flavor and a tender texture. It is a signature dish of the state of Texas.

LOBSTER ROLL

LOBSTER ROLL 🦞

Lobster Roll is a popular seafood sandwich from the northeastern United States, especially Portland (Maine). It is made with fresh lobster meat mixed with butter or mayonnaise and served in a toasted bun.
